About 1v1 BattleGrounds

1v1 BattleGrounds is an intense fighting game where players duel it out in a combat arena. Players can choose between a revolver or a knife to battle, and after each win, they earn coins to unlock new, better weapons. Fight against random opponents or challenge friends!
