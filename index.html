About 1v1 BattleGrounds

In 1v1 BattleGrounds, you will be facing opponents in an intense 1v1 fight inside an arena. Choose your weapon, whether it’s a revolver or a knife, and battle to earn rewards. With every win, you’ll collect coins that can be used to unlock new, powerful weapons.

The goal is to outsmart and outgun your opponent. Can you dominate the arena and become the ultimate champion? Join now and prove your skill in 1v1 battles!
